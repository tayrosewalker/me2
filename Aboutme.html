About Me

A Performing Artist

Taylor-Rose Walker...

is originally from Westford, MA where she began training at her family-owned dance studio. She grew up competing in ballet, jazz, tap, hip hop, etc. She discovered choreography as a passion in her later high school years and decided to pursue both performance and choreography at NYU Tisch School of the Arts. In 2018, Taylor-Rose will recieve her BFA in dance and continue to pursue her dance career in NYC.
